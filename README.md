# Hi there, I'm Ninad Bhat

## About Me

I'm an AI Engineer at [Tomoro.ai](https://tomoro.ai/), where I build applied AI systems for real-world business problems, combining LLMs, machine learning, evaluation, and product engineering.

Previously, I was a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at [Springboards.ai](https://springboards.ai/), where I led machine learning work across two major creative AI initiatives: [Flint Alpha](https://springboards.ai/models/flint-alpha) and the [Creativity Benchmark](https://creativitybenchmark.ai/). My work focused on building and evaluating LLM systems for creative workflows, including model benchmarking, fine-tuning, embedding-based diversity analysis, human preference evaluation, and production integration.

Before industry, I completed a PhD in Applied Machine Learning at the Australian National University, where I built interpretable machine learning tools for materials design and inverse alloy discovery. I've also worked as a Software Engineer at the [Software Innovation Institute](https://sii.anu.edu.au/) and [Instaclustr by NetApp](https://www.instaclustr.com/), and held research roles at [IAS-9, Forschungszentrum Jülich](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/ias/ias-9) in Germany and [Nanocube](https://www.nanocube.com.au/) in Australia.

I hold a Bachelor's and Master's degree from IIT Bombay.
